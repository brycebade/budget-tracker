# Personal Financial Planner

## Guiding Principle

Financial clarity leads to financial confidence.

### Vision

Help people understand, improve, and plan their financial future through clear financial insights, intelligent recommendations, and meaningful visualizations.

The application should not simply record transactions. It should answer:

- Where am I financially?
- Where is my money?
- Where am I headed?
- What should I do next?

A read-only personal financial planning and decision-support platform.

## User Control and Financial Safety

The application is a financial guidance platform, not a financial control platform.

It may collect or receive read-only financial data, analyze that data, model potential outcomes, and recommend actions. It must not move money, execute trades, pay bills, or make irreversible financial decisions on the user's behalf.

The application looks through the financial window. The user remains the only person who can reach through it.

## Product Goals

### Core Principles

1. Financial clarity leads to financial confidence.

2. Every screen should help users make better financial decisions.

3. Information should be easy to understand in under 10 seconds.

4. Defaults should be intelligent.

5. The app guides our financial decisions. It does not conrol your money.

### Goals

- Replace spreadsheets
- Provide a complete financial picture
- Track progress over time
- Encourage better financial habits
- Make finances understandable without accounting knowledge

## Dashboard Philosphy

Dashboard = Financial Command Center

The dashboard is not a list of data.

The dashboard answers four questions.

1. Where am I?

- Net Worth
- Available Cash
- Savings & Investments
- Total Debt
- Financial Health

2. Where is my money?

- Accounts
- Spending
- Investments

3. Where am I going?

- Net Worth Trend
- Debt Trend
- Savings Trend

4. What should I do next?

- Upcoming Bills
- Financial Insights
- Recommendations
